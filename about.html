About 100SMILES Dental Care

100SMILES Dental Care is a modern dental care facility located at 33 Fantail St. in Razole. Our roots in the community run deep, having served our patients for over three decades.

With over 50 years of collective experience in dental care, we pride ourselves on offering friendly, reliable, and compassionate services. Our unwavering dedication is to provide the best possible dental care for all our patients.

We understand the diverse needs of our clients, and that's why we offer a comprehensive range of dental services. We provide a full spectrum of preventive and restorative care, including routine wellness check-ups, dental implants, orthodontics, and cosmetic dentistry. Additionally, we prioritize patient education, ensuring you have the knowledge and resources to make informed decisions about your dental health.
